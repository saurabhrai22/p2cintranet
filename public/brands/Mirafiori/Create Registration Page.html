1. SCOPE

    The goal of this document is to provide the basic tips in order to create Registration page in the Mirafiori Outlet portal.

2. HOW TO CREATE THE REGISTRATION PAGE

    In order to create the Registration Page, follow these steps:

  1. Select the language page (e.g. mirafiorioutlet/mirafiorioutlet-it/it)
  2. Click: Create > Create Page
  3. Choose “Mirafiori Outlet Registration Template” as page layout
  4. Click Next
  5. Insert page data (a title can be “registrazione”)

  6. Click “Create”;
  7. Click “Done” in the opened popup 

3. HOW TO CONFIGURE THE REGISTRATION PAGE

    In order to configure the Registration page follow these steps:

  1. Select the “registration” page (e.g. mirafiorioutlet/mirafiorioutlet-it/it/registrazione)
  2. Click “Properties”
  3. In the “REGISTER” tab configure these fields:

Banner
  • Title: the title of banner section;
  • Subtitle: the subtitle of banner section;
  • Description: the description of banner section;
  • Background image: the background image path of banner section;
Social area
  • Title: the title of social area section;
  • Subtitle: the subtitle of social area section;
Complete Profile Area
  • Title: the title of profile area section;
  • Subtitle: the subtitle of profile area section;
  • CTA label: the CTA label of profile area section;
Registration area
  • Private label: the private label of registration area section;
  • Company label: the company label of registration area section;
  • First name placeholder: the first name placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Last name placeholder: the last name placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Fiscal code placeholder: the fiscal code placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Fiscal code company placeholder: the fiscal code company placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Company name placeholder: the company name placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Email placeholder: the email placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Email confirm placeholder: the email confirm placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Phone 1 placeholder: the phone 1 placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Mobile 1 placeholder: the mobile 1 placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Fax placeholder: the fax placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Vat number placeholder: the vat number placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Gender label: the gender label of registration area section;
  • Password placeholder: the password placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Password confirm placeholder: the password confirm placeholder label of registration area section;
  • Confirm CTA label: the confirm CTA label button of registration area section;
  • Password specs: the password specs label of registration area section;
  • Male label: the male label of registration area section;
  • Female label: the female label of registration area section;
  • Success message: the success message text of registration area section;
  • Already registered title: the already registered title of registration area section;
  • Server error title: the server error title of registration area section;
  • Complete account title: the complete account title of registration area section;
  • Complete account message: the complete account message of registration area section;
  • Ok button label: the ok button label of registration area section;



    In order to complete configuration of the Registration page follow these steps:

  1. Select the “it” page (e.g. mirafiorioutlet/mirafiorioutlet -it/it)
  2. Click “Properties”
  3. In the “FORM GENERAL” tab configure these fields:

Privacy
  • contactFormInformativaTitle: the title of contact form informativa;
  • contactFormInformativaSubtitle1: the subtitle1 of contact form informativa;
  • contactFormInformativaSubtitleLink: the subtitle link of contact form informativa;
  • contactFormInformativaSubtitle2: the subtitle 2 of contact form informativa;
  • Agree: the agree label of the privacy section;
  • Do not agree: the do not agree label of the privacy section;
  • privacymarketing: the privacy marketing label;
  • privacymarketingdescription: the privacy marketing description;
  • privacyprofilation: the privacy profilation label;
  • privacyprofilationdescription: the privacy profilation description;
  • privacythirdpart: the privacy third part label;
  • privacythirdpartdescription: the privacy third part description;
  • Send message: the send message text;
