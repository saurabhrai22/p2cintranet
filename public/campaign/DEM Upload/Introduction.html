Introduction

DEMs(Digital Electronic Messaging) are the emailers used for marketing of a product/service or commercial communication playing a vital role in digital marketing. These emailers give a brief idea about a particular product/service (e.g. various offers on car models, Launch events/anniversaries etc) and specific details are further viewed by opening various links present in the emailers which redirect a user to a landing page, company or brand website, event pages etc.
