DEM QC and Send Test DEM to Market

Once after  successfully uploading the DEM you need to follow the below steps before sending the Test Mails to Market

  1. Take the Test DEM by using Test content in the Actions tab
  2. Check how the Emailer is looking in the outlook and the browser. If there is any issue, check if is it fixable or not. You can refer to @Bocaletti.Fabio mail for fixing some common issues.
  3. Once everything is fine, perform the self QC as per Checklist and share the DEM to QC team for testing
  4. Finally QC team will test the Emailer and comment the details on ticket. Then you need to send the test DEM to the related market as per Campaing_creation_guidelines file
