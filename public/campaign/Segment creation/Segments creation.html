Segment Creation

  1. Go to the home page, click on the 4th tab of Audience and select Segments.


  2. Click on create a segment.


  3. Click on the blank segment.


  4. To build your segment, in the left pane, click Add. Then click one of the following options. The one’s highlighted are widely used by us.


    • Filter criteria: Filter contacts based on implicit or explicit data about the contact. For example, filter for a particular market. Or filter based on contact or account fields. Filters are a powerful component of segmentation and you can include multiple filters to make sure you reach your target audience.
    • Individual contacts: Add individual contacts from your database.
    • Shared filter or shared list: Use a shared filter or list available to you.


  5. Click on the plus sign on the left-hand side and select Filter criteria.


  6. Drag the filter criteria given on the right side to the board in middle.


  7. Double click on the compare contact field to select the required contact field (For eg: COD_Market) and then select the country you are working on (For eg: Italy.


  8. This way you can select multiple contact fields that is required in your campaign separated by “AND” filter.


  9. Apart from the contact fields, we are supposed to take “Custom Object” field. This “Custom Object” is different for each target group.For example, if we are targeting customers, the CO (Custom Object) will be “CAR”. The Car CO will have all the details that is needed for targeting..

    Drag the below highlighted filter criteria on the middle board



    10. Double click on the custom object filter and select the “CAR” CO (as we have considered “customers” as target group.



    By clicking on the Add button, we can add required filters.



  10. You can also add shared filters if any (For eg: Flag seed).




    11. It will appear under the filter criteria as shown in the below screen grab.



  11. Once all the filters have been selected, click on the “Save” button on the right to get counts.
