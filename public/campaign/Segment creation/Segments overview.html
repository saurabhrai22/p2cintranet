Segments Overview

Segments are groups of contacts generated based on filter criteria and contact lists. Segments can filter contacts based on criteria like if they own a particular brand, whether or not they requested for a newsletter, whether or not expressed their interest to buy a car or whether or not they opened/clicked the previous email sent.

Segments are used to feed email distribution in campaigns. They allow you to specify which contacts are included, and then to customize the subsequent actions for those contacts.


Examples

Segments can be very general, like people who have given permission to send newsletters, belong to a particular group (Jeep Owner’s Group), registered on a particular date or belong to particular gender etc. Create segments that are appropriate for your client’s needs.
