Segmentation Rules

  1. There are six default golden conditions/criteria to be applied on every campaign. They are:-
    • Privacy should be “G”
    • Email address shouldn’t be blank
    • Flag only dealer email should be “N” (that means people who wants only local dealer to contact them shouldn’t be targeted)
    • No hardbounce
    • COD market
    • Record validity should be “Y”.

  3. Prospect segment will never have any Custom object filter, instead will have an extra contact filter i.e Newsletter_Brand = “G”.
  4. There cannot be more than 15 fields on the segmentation board.
