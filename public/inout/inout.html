Out of scope activities management

In next slides we present changes that are out of scope for each brand – cannot be done as a standard ticket following standard P2C process. This is from 3 reasons:

Internal ACN dependency on the development Teams (FiatSupport or Eloqua

These teams are part of Accenture but not part of P2C project and they take longer response times. We use a tool Bug Tracker to contact them.

External 3rd party dependency

The given part of the website is managed by an external 3rd party agency or tool. In this case local FCA directly or LAL as facilitation can contact the 3rd part agncy and request the changes.

Change Request scope and procedure

Some changes if requested, will require a Change Request procedure including HQ approval, Fiatsupport Team technical approval and additional cost to the local market. Also any other not presented here changes that will be evaluated as CR will be subject to this procedure. Details about the CR procedure can be found in the presentation:
