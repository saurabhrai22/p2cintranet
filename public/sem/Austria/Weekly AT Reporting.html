    1. Delete previous week properly without deleting formulas.

      • Leads
      • Engagements
      • Clicks
      • CTR

      1. Custom filter (Largest to Smallest) for:
        1. Leads (Quotation Request + Test Drive)
        2. Engagements (rest all conversions)
      2. CTR (Largest to Smallest of Account)
      3.   CPC (Smallest to Largest of Account)
