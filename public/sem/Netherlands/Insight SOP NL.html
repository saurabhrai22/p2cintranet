Below are the observations of Netherland market insight:
  1. Kindly observe main metrics carefully in “Weekly” tab i. e. Spends, Clicks & Cost per click etc.
  2. Try to find co-relation between changes observed in the report then please check change history in AdWords to get better idea on what happened in last week.
  3. Please check for any Promotional Activities held in last week through “Search Trend Report” or can confirm with Market Lead.
  4. Before starting the insights, paste WoW formulas for Channel, Brand Type as well as Model tabs. This comparison helps to clear the picture about the insights to be noted – whether rise or fall, their percentages, which was the top performing model etc.
  5. To begin with, first point will always mention the drops or gains for Spends, traffic and cost per click and their relationship – percentage as well as in currency. This point also mentions about the overall budget amount spent till the reporting date with the use of Over-Under spend calculator (Calculated using Quarterly Budget).
  6. Second point mainly focuses on rise and fall in Lead and cost per lead and more focus will be on the highest or lowest performing models. Please check for tracking parameter issues.
  7. Third point will be, none the less but Engagements – rise and fall, best or worst conversion types on Models and campaigns.
  8. Remarketing mainly focuses on RLSA users – their WoW performance for lead generations and their co-relations with CR (Lead rate) and CPL (Cost per lead) against standard users.
  9. Brand Impressions Share highlighting Search IS and Search Lost IS Budget on WoW basis for Brand Type and Exact match keywords and Best performing Models for highest Search IS.
  10. Last but not the least, use of Reverso tool to check whether your insights are grammatically correct or not before sending out to the on shore.
