  1. What needs to be changed for Raw data (weekly/monthly) –

** After downloading verify date range and Scope should be Entire Account, with Group Devices report, Keywords Report and Creatives Report (Scroll and Check) –  Campaigns doesn't contain remarketing or GDN or DR.

  1. Delete 0 Impressions from both the Reports – Group/Campaign Device and Keywords.

    2. ** Fiat Brand: There are 2 keywords raw data and out of which the 1st one is usually in reduced dimension, and you need to delete that data and format the later one.

  2. Sequence for Raw Data will be- Group/Campaign Device and Keywords.
  3. Run the Automation (no filters should be applied and close all other active Excels).
  4. Complete the mapping if there are “Not Found”.
  5. Run the Automation again (no filters should be applied and close all other active Excels)
  6. Tally numbers with Dashboard.
  7. Once the automation is successfully completed we need to update the conversion types number at the historical data for previous week.
  8. Update IS for models from GDS Dashboard and refer Google Ads for Conquer and Attack IS for Fiat.
  9. Remarketing data will be from Google Ads (both Remarketing and Totals)
    1. Audience Tab: Filter - Audience Type-  contains -How they have interacted with your business {select all types} – Filtered campaigns
      1. Data - Clicks, Impression, Spends, Quotation Request, Test Drive and Contact Request
    2. Audience Tab: Search Campaigns
      1. Data - Clicks, Impression, Spends, Quotation Request, Test Drive and Contact Request
  10. Group the weekly tab to display 12 weeks data.
  11. Delete the zeros from Conversion types for Weekly, Channel, Brand Type and Top Keywords Tab.
  12. If there are no leads at the Channel, Brand Type and Top Keywords tabs then delete data related to it.
  13. Including clicks, impression, etc., if '0' will be deleted for Channel and Brand Type.
  14. Adjust the charts so that they do not hide the reporting table.
  15. **Summary Reports will be updated from the Macro Automations.
  16. **Summary sequence and pattern to be followed as BE- SOP - Weekly Insights. Pointers related to Marginal or nominal changes can be skipped.
