France Weekly Insights Standard Operating Procedure

  • The Insight Sample that we Sent to the Client:

 

  • Spends marginally declined by -5% to €2,144.48 in line with lesser traffic (-7%) and stable cost per click to €0.24, mainly on Tipo and Brand generic campaigns resulted stable impressions (+2%) which affected overall click through rate of the account by -8% to 15.37%.
  • Conversion volume level up despite lesser traffic on the account from 7 to 8, lowering cost per conversion (€268.06; -17%) and led to improve conversion rate (0.09% vs 0.07%; +22%.
  • Remarketing users used 32% of the overall traffic share but lead conversion volume went down from 6 to 2, increasing cost per lead and cost per conversion from €131.54 to €418.85.
  • Channel wise, Desktop acquired maximum conversions 6 vs. 4 last week, while conversions on Mobile remained stable at 2, Tablet (0 vs 1) failed to make any conversion this week.
  • At model level, Punto is the only model which received 1 conversion. Surprisingly, rest all conversions recorded from Fiat Generic Keywords (7). Due to ending of promotions on Tipo (0 vs. 1) received less traffic and did not convert this week. Even traffic gained on Panda (0 vs. 1) but did not acquire conversion this week.
  • Traffic increases by +48% on Non-Brand keywords, along with Spends. No conversion recorded on the account this week. Impression share increased (+10%) from 25.24% to 27.68% as visibility lost due to budget increase to 67.66% from 0.01% this week, while impression share lost due to rank declined (-94%) to 4.66% from 74.75%.
  • Overall impression share declined (-6%) to 47.88% from 50.87% as result of massive increment in loss of impression share due to budget by 12445% from 0.40% to 50.18%. Visibility loss due to low rank declined (-96%) to 1.94% from 48.73%.

 

             Note: Before start insights remember that we should focus mainly on leads and CPL

             What are the bullets points an insight should carry?

  • As the reports is having tab names as (weekly, channel, Brand Type, Model, Remarketing) our insights must elaborate on the same lines. Apart from this also include detailed insights for Non-Brand Keywords and Impression share for the account.
  • Weekly tab describes all the major preforming matrices and their correlations. For e.g CTR = Clicks / Impression, CPC = Cost / Click).
  • Channel tab mainly focus on devices wise data on account.
  • In brand tab we mainly focus on brand type. Though we mention Non-brand related keywords in sperate bullet points.
  • For Model tab we must compare the models only.
  • Remarketing tab we must mention about only related to remarketing.
  • And one bullet point for Non-Brand and a separate point for Impression share.

 

  • Points to be remembered:

 

  1. 1st point: - In weekly tab Carefully observe the main metrics on the and begin with drop or gains – Spends, Traffic (we must have to mention for which model traffic increases or decrease), CPC and CTR – trying to find their co-relations.

Note: - Before we touch down to the rest of the points we must apply WoW comparison ((this week – last week)/last week) to all tabs. This comparison helps to clear the picture about the insights to be noted – whether rise or fall, their percentages, which was the top performing model etc.

 

  1. 2nd Point: - Observe weekly tab carefully and focus mainly on Leads and Engagements whether rise or fall and Because of that what are the parameters affected. The parameter such as (p1-cost/lead, p2- cost/engagements, p3 – lead CR, p4 – Engagement CR).
  2. 3rd point: - Remarketing always to be begin with, drops or gains in terms traffic and leads or else what are the sharing percentage from total side by side their relationship with cost per lead – percentage as well as in currency.
  3. 4th point: - In this point we surely focus on channels and mention from highest and lowest performing devices in terms conversion (leads (always compare to the last week and try to mention in numerical format only) + engagements (just mention the how many engagements obtained and the percentile share of engagements from total) compare to the traffic variation with respect to the devices.
  4. 5th Point: - Here in Model tab, major focus will be on the highest or lowest performing models with respect to the traffic and try to write it down at what cost that we got this amount of leads and engagements (Cost/engagements, cost/leads).and percentile variation of leads and engagements compare to the last week (if need).
  5. 6th points: - For this point we must look at brand type tab and explains about only Non-brand keywords like what is the status of traffic and CPC compared to the last week (increasing or else decreasing) and because of that impact on spends and most important mention about leads and engagements at what cost acquire those (if needed cost/lead, cost/engagements)
  6. 7th point: - In Last point we must write about the impression share (search IS) and search lost IS budget, Search lost IS rank.

    1. What need to be changed for Raw data –
      1. Sort Marin Raw data – Remove zero Impressions from Group Devices Data and Keywords (P.S. Check for Impressions count and clicks, cost). Ensure that the metrics in the excel downloaded from Marin reflect AdWords metrics on dashboard. In case of any discrepancy then please escalate the issue with Onshore team.
      2. For Keywords device raw data remove the zero impressions and save the raw data.

 

  1. Automation running procedure:
    1. Open automation -> Go to device raw data -> Click on the run button -> Ask for date range fill the data rage like (Apr 02- Apr 08) -> then again fill the date like month/date – month/date(e.g 04/02-04/08) -> Ask for raw data for that particular brand (please select that raw data) -> Ask for you want to save the raw data or not (please select no) -> if non found campaigns there in the device raw data then map those campaigns in the mapping tab and save it -> close the automation -> again open the automation and follow the above steps -> after completion the above steps automation ask for reports please select previous week report which we send to the client -> after data fetching done save the reports and automation.

 

Note: For mapping follow as per the bellow table:

 

Campaign Name Brand Type Model Type

After mapping “GNRC” – rest all are Brand, so finish brand type mapping (Brand Name) Brand NA
Apply custom filter at campaign -> contains “Model and Model Name” Ex. Contains “Mode and Renegade” Brand Renegade
Once finished mapping all models and still you find unmapped campaigns, map them to respective (brand Generic) Brand Jeep Brand
