• Below is the sample insight for Fiat Brand (insights for other brands will be in the same format):

FIAT

  • Traffic for the brand grew +14% with -7% reduced cost per click, thus increasing the spends by +6% to DKK 15,484.33. There was only +7% growth in click through rate, with increased traffic and visibility. So far, 57% from DKK 66,667.00 of the March monthly budget has been spent.
  • Conversions trend to drop WoW from 9 to 6 to 3 with loses in Quotation Request, hiking the cost per conversion +from DKK 1,608.92 to DKK 2,428.03 to DKK 5,161.44.
  • Channel-wise, Desktop acquired the 3 conversions while mobile and tablet device lost their conversions.
  • Model-wise, only 500L was performing with 1 conversion at a cost per conversion of DKK 488.21. Out of 3 conversions, brand keywords contributed 2 conversions vs., 1 last week.
  • The loss to the overall conversions was most impacted due to loss of conversions from remarketing users. The conversions for Quotation Request fall from 4 to 1 and nil conversions for Test drive despite of increased traffic share.

Brand search impression share increase to 90% with no loss of impressions due to budget.

JEEP

  • Traffic leveled up +58% impacted by Compass model with -29% reduced cost per click, increased the spends +12% to DKK 1,942.73. Click through rate shoot up +43% with drastic change to traffic and relatively slight change to visibility. The brand has spent 53% of DKK 8,033.00 from the March budget till date.
  • Despite of growth in traffic, conversions stand stable at 4 increasing the cost per conversion +12% to DKK 485.68.
  • Chanel Wise, Conversions from desktop users drop from 4 to 3 while mobile users gain 1 conversion this week at a cost per conversion of DKK 694.60.
  • Model Wise, Compass loses its conversions from 3 to 1 increasing the cost per conversion despite of increased traffic. On the hand, brand keywords gain conversions from 1 to 3 with marginal change to the traffic reducing the overhead cost for per conversion.
  • Conversions for remarketing users doubles from 1 to 2 with increased traffic share at a -11% reduced cost per conversion.
  • The impression share for brand campaigns increases to 46% with increased loss of impressions due to budget reduces to 38%.

 

  • Points that will help to write insights:
  1. Evaluate the reports – starting with the leads > relating it to the change in traffic > changes noted in cost per click > how it has affected the Spends and ultimately the cost per lead.
  2. Observe the %change {formula: =(current week value - last week/last week)} and difference in numbers WoW for all the tabs – brand type wise, remarketing stage and model wise – as one or more of those might have affected the changes
  3. Point number 2 will roughly give an idea over the model which has affected. To find the root cause - apply comparison with the last week to find the exact campaign.
  4. Check the Graph representation to know the date on which the changes were affected
  5. Change history will help to know the tentative reason that would have affected the changes
    1. Even Google Trends help to know the reason if not supported by Change history
    2. Search term report will give keywords that were out of the campaign focus yet getting clicks and increasing the CPC
  6. Briefing of insights –
    1. Starting with the change observed in spends with relation to traffic (clicks) and cost per click and later for click through which is co-related to visibility (impressions). The 1st point will also support the utilization of monthly budget calculated in the DK - Over-Under Spend Calculator_(Day wise) report
    2. 2nd Point will have the changes in conversions - effect on CPL – change in conversion rate if required co-relation to traffic also can be mentioned
    3. 3rd Point – Channel wise changes to be mentioned in terms of conversions (always write about the device that recorded highest conversions 1st) – if no conversions were recorded then change in CTR and CPC of all the devices to be scripted
    4. 4th point same as third but for Models – if all models have had conversions; we can mention the ones that affected the most and latter comment for brand generic and non-brand
    5. 5th point is for remarketing users – the point will be supported highlighting the leads if any in relation to the traffic; if no leads then to comment on the changes in CPC and CTR
    6. Last point to be jotted for the Impression Share of Brand Campaigns and the effect from the IS lost by budget.

**As far as possible write the positive points first. While mentioning the change in percentage apply – or + symbols to define the trend.
